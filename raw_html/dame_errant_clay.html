Discover, Learn, Grow: Join Our Upcoming Workshops

Workshop Calendar

Please Note:

Doors open 15 minutes before workshop begins and we start right on time. If you are coming with a group we encourage you to come 15 minutes early if you want to sit together.

  • Standardly takes 2-3 weeks. Check up dates at our firing updates tab.

    Pieces are held for 8 weeks from creation date.

  • These vary depending on prep needed:

    Sphere based workshops-30 minutes

    Cured slab workshops-minimum of 3 hours

    Carve workshops 5-38 hours depending on when we throw.

    If the minimum attendance is not met the workshop will be canceled. It is always a good idea to register early!

  • Please check our FAQ for our full cancelation policy

Explore Our Diverse Workshop Offerings

Whether you're looking to enhance your skills, explore new interests, or improve your well-being, our workshops offer something for everyone. Led by experienced professionals, each session is crafted to inspire creativity and provide hands-on experience in a supportive and engaging environment.
